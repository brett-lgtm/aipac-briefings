About AIPAC

The American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) is a bipartisan pro-Israel advocacy organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship. For over 60 years, AIPAC has worked to ensure that America's support for Israel is both strong and enduring.

AIPAC educates members of Congress, the Executive Branch, and the American public about the importance of the U.S.-Israel relationship. Through policy briefings, advocacy campaigns, and grassroots mobilization, AIPAC ensures that pro-Israel voices are heard in Washington and across America.

This briefing series is part of AIPAC's commitment to educating Americans about critical threats to Israel and the Middle East, and empowering supporters to take action.
